Content Management Platforms

Managing your nonprofit's collaborative content is stressful enough, so let open source do the hard work while you focus on having social impact. Here are some of the most well-known open source content management platforms:

Drupal


https://www.drupal.org/

Intuitive, scalable, secure, and equipped with many powerful features, Drupal is trusted by companies, governments, universities, and nonprofits the world over.

Joomla


https://www.joomla.org/

This popular open source content management system boasts features like RSS feeds, blogging, page caching, content management, contact management, media management, banner management, search capability, and multilingual support.

Django


https://www.djangoproject.com/

Fast, intuitive, secure, and scalable open source content management system based on the Python Web framework.

WordPress


https://wordpress.com/

This leading content management system is available for use as a downloadable software package or as a hosting service for your nonprofit's website or blog.
